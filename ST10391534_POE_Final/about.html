About Us

Founded in 2023, The Petting Zoo Vet Clinic is a compassionate and dedicated veterinary practice with a profound mission at its heart. We are committed to providing welfare veterinary care to sick and suffering animals living within townships and impoverished communities.

At The Petting Zoo, we believe that every animal, regardless of their circumstances, deserves access to high-quality veterinary care and a chance at a healthier, happier life. Our team of skilled and caring veterinarians, technicians, and support staff work tirelessly to fulfill this mission by offering a range of services tailored to the unique needs of our patients. We understand the challenges faced by animals in underserved areas and are driven by a deep sense of responsibility to make a positive impact on their lives. Our clinic is not just a place of healing; it's a beacon of hope for animals in need.

Through our unwavering commitment to animal welfare, community engagement, and education, The Petting Zoo Vet Clinic aims to uplift the lives of both animals and their human companions. We aspire to create a world where no animal is left to suffer due to lack of resources or care.

Join us in our mission to provide love, care, and healing to the animals that depend on us, and together, let's make a difference in the lives of those who need it most.
